Privacy Policy

Your privacy and confidentiality are our top priorities

Last updated: {{ "now"|date:"F j, Y" }}
Important: Moodigo is designed with privacy-first principles. We collect minimal data, use anonymous sessions, and prioritize your confidentiality above all else.

1. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information:
  • Anonymous session identifiers (not linked to personal identity)
  • Chat messages and mood entries (stored anonymously)
  • Basic usage analytics (page views, feature usage)
  • Technical information (browser type, device type for optimization)
Information You Provide:
  • Chat messages and conversations with Moodigo
  • Mood tracker entries and notes
  • Mental health assessment responses
  • Optional preferences (preferred name, university - if provided)
We DO NOT collect: Real names, email addresses, phone numbers, IP addresses, or any personally identifiable information unless you explicitly provide it.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Support: To offer personalized mental health guidance and responses
  • Improve Service: To enhance Moodigo's AI models and user experience (using anonymized data)
  • Safety Monitoring: To detect crisis situations and provide appropriate resources
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve our services (aggregate data only)

3. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Anonymous Storage: Personal conversations are not linked to identifiable information
  • Secure Servers: Data is stored on secure, monitored servers
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel can access anonymized data for service improvement

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to prevent harm
  • Emergency Situations: If there's an imminent threat to safety (following legal protocols)
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate the service (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Your Rights

  • Access: Request information about data we have (though most is anonymous)
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Correction: Update or correct any information
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a readable format

6. Data Retention

  • Chat conversations: Retained for 30 days, then anonymized for service improvement
  • Mood data: Retained while you use the service, deleted upon request
  • Assessment data: Retained for research purposes in anonymized form
  • Usage analytics: Retained in aggregate form indefinitely

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Session Cookies: To maintain your chat session (essential for service)
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics: Basic usage analytics to improve our service (anonymized)

8. Third-Party Services

Moodigo may integrate with:

  • Crisis hotlines and mental health resources (links only)
  • Analytics services (with anonymized data)
  • Cloud hosting providers (with encryption and security agreements)

9. Age Requirements

Moodigo is intended for users 13 years and older. If you are under 18, please consider involving a parent, guardian, or trusted adult in your mental health journey.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated through the platform. Continued use constitutes acceptance of updated terms.

11. Contact Us

Questions about privacy? We're here to help. While we don't have a traditional contact form, you can discuss privacy concerns with Moodigo directly in the chat interface.

Our Commitment to You

Your mental health journey is deeply personal. We're committed to protecting your privacy while providing the support you need. Your trust is essential to our mission of helping students achieve better mental health.
