How Moodigo Works

Simple steps to better mental health

1. Start a Conversation Anytime

Click the chat button, and Moodigo will greet you with a warm, non-judgmental tone. Whether you're feeling anxious, stressed, lonely, or just need to vent â€” Moodigo is here.

2. Real-Time Support

Moodigo uses AI and natural language understanding to respond thoughtfully. It will ask how you're feeling, suggest calming techniques, share motivational quotes, and help you reflect and reframe negative thoughts.

3. Mood Tracking & Check-Ins

Moodigo can perform daily or weekly check-ins to track your emotional patterns. You'll get visual insights and personalized tips for maintaining emotional balance.

4. Resource Guidance

When needed, Moodigo will recommend university counseling services, mental health hotlines, breathing and meditation apps, and crisis support resources.

5. Confidential & Secure

Your privacy matters. All chats with Moodigo are encrypted and confidential. You can chat anonymously, without fear of judgment.

The Technology Behind Moodigo

Advanced AI Models

Moodigo uses state-of-the-art machine learning models trained specifically for mental health support. Our models can detect emotional states, assess risk levels, and provide appropriate responses based on established psychological principles.

We implement privacy-first architecture with end-to-end encryption, anonymous sessions, and secure data handling. Your conversations are never stored in identifiable ways and are protected by industry-standard security measures.

Continuous Learning

Our AI models continuously improve through anonymized interactions, ensuring better support quality over time. We regularly update our knowledge base with the latest mental health research and best practices.
